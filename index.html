⚠️ Important: If you're experiencing "400 Bad Request" errors when watching series, please clear your browser cookies and cache. This error is caused by cookies exceeding size limits. Click here for instructions.

How to Fix the 400 Bad Request

Easy Step

  1. Play any Movie
  2. Go back to main page
  3. Now play series
  4. It Works now

On Chrome (Desktop):

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  2. Set time range to "All time"
  3. Check "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files"
  4. Click "Clear data"
  5. Restart your browser

On Chrome (Mobile):

  1. Tap the three dots menu → Settings
  2. Go to Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data
  3. Check "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files"
  4. Set time range to "All time"
  5. Tap "Clear data"

On Safari (iOS):

  1. Go to Settings → Safari
  2. Tap "Clear History and Website Data"
  3. Confirm by tapping "Clear"

Alternative: Try Private/Incognito Mode

Opening the site in a private/incognito window may also solve the issue without clearing cookies.
